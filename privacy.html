Our Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters deeply to us at globexoa.site.

This policy explains how we gather, utilize, and safeguard your information while you explore our website, https://globexoa.site/ (referred to as "the Site"). By accessing this Site, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, contact number, billing/shipping details, and payment information.

Non-Personal Information: Covers details such as browser type, device details, IP address, browsing activity, and location data.

Payment Information: Payments are handled through secure third-party processors like PayPal and Stripe. Your full payment details are not stored by us.

2. How We Use Your Data

To manage and complete your orders, including processing payments and delivering products.

To provide order confirmations, delivery updates, and other transactional notifications.

To send promotional content, discounts, or updates (only with your permission).

To enhance our customer service, website functionality, and your shopping experience.

To address your inquiries and deliver assistance.

To comply with legal regulations or enforce our terms of use.

3. Cookies and Tracking Tools

Cookies and similar tools are used to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device, allowing us to remember preferences, track shopping carts, and analyze website usage. You can adjust your browser settings to manage or disable cookies, but doing so might limit some features of the Site.

4. Third-Party Services

We collaborate with third-party services for various operations, such as payment processing, analytics, and advertising. These entities may handle your data according to their own privacy policies.

Payment Providers: Stripe, PayPal, etc.

Analytics Tools: Google Analytics, etc.

Advertising Platforms: Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for detailed information on data usage.

5. Data Security Measures

We employ robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to secure your personal data. While we strive for optimal protection, no system is entirely immune to vulnerabilities. We cannot provide an absolute guarantee of data security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • The right to access and obtain copies of your data.
  • The right to request corrections for inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to restrict or object to certain data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at [insert email address]. We aim to respond promptly to such requests.

7. Protecting Children

Our website is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals below this age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, reach out to us immediately, and we will remove the information promptly.

8. Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. When changes are made, the updated policy will be available on this page, along with the revised "Effective Date." We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed about how your data is managed.
